Executive and VIP Protection Bodyguard Services

Hire Executive and VIP Protection Bodyguard Services. Distinguished people require constant security service in order to maintain their privacy and protect their reputation.

 
 

Northern Force Security provides the following Executive and VIP security services for private and public individuals.

 

Escort

You may feel safe and secure while you are on your way to a shareholders meeting, corporate event, trade show or personal matter. However, you may be followed or watched and your privacy and security may be put at risk. Northern Force Security professional escort guards are trained to make your route safe and secure.

 

Safe route

Your transportation and travel itinerary must be thoroughly planned and designed in order to maintain the highest level of individual security. Your driver may be the most experienced in different traffic situations but your journey must be safe and planned. Northern Force Security professional experts will provide you with safe route planning ensuring your safety and privacy is a top priority.

 

Inspection and examination

Your destination must be thoroughly examined before your arrival in order to prevent potential risk to your safety and to maintain your privacy and security. Northern Force Security professional experts will inspect all vulnerable spots as part of our VIP/Executive services so as to allow you to focus on your priorities.

 

Drivers

Your driver is undoubtedly a great asset. There are situations however, when you require a higher level of professional drivers services. Northern Force Security’s professional drivers will transport you in a safe and secure manner paying increased attention to all threatening situations. Our drivers are available on a full-time or occasional basis.
